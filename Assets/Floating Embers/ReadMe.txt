-------------------
Floating Embers
-------------------

Floating Embers is a collection of embers particle effects. You can easily use them to add atmosphere to your environment. You can also use them as menu backgrounds for action/war/RPG games.

Contains:
- 22 ember shapes. 
- 1 demo scene.
- 14 embers prefabs
- 7 Textures.

WindZone is supported. You may use the WindZone's direction and strength to control the direction of embers' movement.
